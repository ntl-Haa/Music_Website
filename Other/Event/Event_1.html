Tomorrowland Reveals Dates and Theme for 2023 Edition

Yesterday is History, Today is a Gift, Tomorrow is Mystery

17 DEC 2022

09:00 GTM+7

1. Skiing & snowboarding

Get ready for a week of skiing, snowboarding and partying on top of the world with Afrojack, Amber Broos, Amelie Lens, ANNA, Dimitri Vegas & Like Mike, Joris Voorn, Kölsch, Lost Frequencies, Mandy, Martin Garrix, Netsky, Ofenbach and many more! Festival visitors will be treated to unforgettable performances throughout the day at different stages in the ski resort and on the mountains, specially created at the foot of the slopes for easy access by ski or snowboard. Alpe d'Huez Grand Domaine Ski is ranked among the top-rated ski resorts in the world, boasting 250 kilometres of slopes and 300 days of sunshine a year with fun for every level of skiers and snowboarders.

'Scenery and Flags'

2. Paragliding

Not everyone can ski or snowboard, but luckily there's a range of great activities for non-skiers as well at Tomorrowland Winter! For the real daredevils out there: there's a spectacular paragliding flight that takes you all the way from the top of the mountain back to the resort with incredible views of the French Alps…

'Skydiving and Air Ballooning'

3. Savoring delicious mountain food

Amidst the beautiful French Alps, Tomorrowland's Tastes of The World cuisine offers you a bunch of unique restaurant experiences, food stands, outdoor barbecues, cheese fondues & raclettes and more than 50 other restaurants & bars in Alpe d'Huez to recharge after a day in the mountains.

'Food'

4. Theme for 2023 edition: 'Adscendo'

Tomorrowland organizers have promised to deliver unparalleled storytelling to complement the theme as well as provide a brand new digital experience at the end of January for all users who pre-register for tickets. While the world awaits more visuals, it's best to leave off with words from the organizers themselves:

'Adscendo' will take people on a beautiful adventure with all kinds of surprises throughout the entire year - more info will follow soon.

'Poster: Tomorrowland for 2023'
